Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is a beloved dish made from pasta, typically boiled to a tender texture and served with a variety of flavorful sauces.
It is often paired with marinara, meat sauce, Alfredo, or pesto, and enhanced with garnishes like Parmesan cheese or fresh herbs.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Ground Meat
  • Tomatoes and Tomato Paste
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Spices

steps

  • Add water, salt, and spaghetti to a pot of water
  • Drain water from pot when the spaghetti is tender
  • Heat oil in a pot
  • Add meat until it is browned
  • Add onion and stir in
  • Add garlic, tomato paste, and spices and stir
  • Add water and tomatoes and stir
  • Let simmer then toss in the cooked pasta
  • Serve
