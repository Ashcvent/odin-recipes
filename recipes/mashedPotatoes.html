Mashed Potatoes

Description

Mashed potatoes are a classic comfort food made from boiled potatoes that are mashed to a smooth, creamy consistency.
Typically prepared with butter, milk or cream, and seasoned with salt and pepper, they have a rich, velvety texture and a slightly savory, buttery flavor.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes
  2. In a pot of water add water, salt, and the potatoes
  3. Boil the potatoes until they are tender
  4. Drain the water
  5. (As needed) Gradually add butter and milk to the pot of potatoes
  6. Mash the potatoes
  7. Serve
